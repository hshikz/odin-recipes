Relationship Disaster-"The ghosting souffle"

"A delicate dish that requires precise timing, emotional unavailability, and the ability to read absolutely zero social cues.
Best prepared when you're 3 months into talking stage, she's sending good morning texts, and you respond with 'lol' and a thumbs up emoji. Serves: 1 (because there's nobody else left)."

Ingredients

  • 1x situationship (ripe, but not committed)
  • 3 red flags (ignored, obviously)
  • 1 attachment style: Avoidant
  • Unlimited "I'm busy" excuses
  • 1 dating app you never deleted (for 'friends')
  • A sprinkle of "we're just vibing"

Steps

  1. Match on a dating app. Exchange memes for 2 weeks. Build zero emotional depth.
  2. Go on one amazing date. Overthink everything. Text your friends 47 screenshots.
  3. She says she really likes you. Panic. Your fight-or-flight activates.
  4. Start responding slower. "Sorry was gaming" at 2pm on a Tuesday.
  5. She confronts you. You say "I'm not ready for anything serious" despite acting serious for 3 months.
  6. She leaves. You're free! ...You're free? ...You're crying to anime OPs at 3am.
  7. Repeat with a new match. The cycle continues. You are the protagonist of your own tragedy.
