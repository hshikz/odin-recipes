AIO Vape Disaster. "The burnt coil catasthrope"

A recipe perfected by every mod or AIO user who forgot to prime their coil.
One wrong move and you're inhaling the taste of regret, burnt cotton, and your dignity evaporating faster than your e-liquid.
Best served with a side of coughing fits and the realization that you just wasted 30k IDR on a coil.

Ingredients

  • 1x brand new 0.8Ω coil (unprimed, obviously)
  • 3ml of your favorite salt nic (50mg, because you hate yourself,and you are a lunatic who doing mtl when you can buy ciggarete)
  • 1x mod or aio system with a fully charged battery (false confidence. and who need watt control when you can hog all that delicious power with your potentiometer mod and your boro mechanical)
  • 0 patience
  • A pinch of "I'll just hit it real quick"

Steps

  1. Install the new coil dry with your fresh wicked cotton
  2. Do NOT add e-liquid to the tank or the cotton. Who reads instructions anyway?
  3. place back your RDA cover and driptip. for aio, put the tank back to its tank then install it to the aio then put the driptip
  4. Fill the tank or drip e-juice to your RDA to the brim. It's not leaking, it's ventilating.
  5. Wait exactly 0 seconds. Prime time is for cowards.
  6. Take a long, deep drag like you're the main character in a cyberpunk anime.
  7. Experience the burnt hit. Cough dramatically. Question your life choices.
  8. Realize you now own a 400K IDR paperweight. Cry in weeb.
